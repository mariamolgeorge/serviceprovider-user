BluSki Facilities Management Services is a partnership firm set up in Thrissur by individuals to cater to the difficulty in sourcing professional maintenance services. The Managing Partner, Ramachandran had been involved in the facility management sector in the U.A.E for over two decades. He is supported by a Working Partner, Sanju, who is experienced in Facilities Management Quality Assurance.

The Bluski team specialises in providing reliable, quality services that meet the specific needs of each of its customers. Facility Management essentially looks after all the building parameters that keeps a space up and running.

BluSki offers facility management is a one stop shop for all home and office maintenance needs. BluSki has employed experts in each of the respective fields to ensure that the services offered are of top quality.

We are proud to provide first-class facilities management services for a whole range of buildings, industries and environments. With our vast experience at home and abroad, you can be sure that your property is in safe hands. Our team will work with you to tailor your building maintenance services to your exact needs, so that you can be sure your property will be in great condition at all times.
